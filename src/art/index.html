This is my VRChat avatar. I used skip4d's Chibi Avatar Base to make it! It was really easy to work with and I highly recommend it if you want to make your own model in the same style.

I made the horns and halo myself using Blender, turned the upright rabbit ears to the side to make cow ears, and modified a glasses model by iPoly3D to fit. It's even "Good" rated on PC and Quest! (which was a lot of work to get right!!!)
